Guinea Pig

Despite their name, they are not pigs, nor do they come from Guinea in Africa! Some think they got their name from the squealing sounds they make.

  • Scientific Name: Cavia porcellus
  • Average Length: 7.9-9.8 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 4-8 years
  • Habitat: Domesticated; no longer wild.

Guinea pigs are tail-less rodents with stout bodies and are kept as pets by many people, including myself! They are gorgeous creatures with soft fur and they rarely bite or scratch, which makes them ideal pets.
